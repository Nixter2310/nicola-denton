Interests

Just for fun!

Photography

I've always loved being creative and photography is a big passion of mine. I used to shoot live music photography for LLTV but now I'm a hobbyist landscape photographer.

Digital Art

I've recently been experimenting with digital art and drawing on my tablet. It's another great way to enjoy being creative and I also find it very therapeutic.

Baking

I've loved baking ever since I was a child when I used to make cakes and scones with my grandad. I'm no Mary Berry but I have plenty of cook books to help me learn! My favourite thing to make is Oreo brownies!

Games

I'm a proud nerd and I love to play video games and tabletop games such as Dungeons and Dragons with my friends. I also like painting the intricate tabletop models.
